Welcome to my blog!

In the rising age of AI, it is more important than ever to have a sharp mind and a solid understanding of what you know and what you are doing. Mathematical problems and puzzles are a great way to maintain and continuously train your brain, improving your problem-solving skills. This blog is dedicated to sharing interesting mathematical problems and puzzles that I have solved—many of which challenged me at first, as the solution is not always straightforward or obvious. I hope whoever reads my posts will find them educational, and take an interest to sharpen their brain to keep it brain-fresh, not brain-rotted.
